Personal Information

I am a Computer science MS student at UNICAMP - University of Campinas where I also majored in Computer Engineering in 1997. My main interest areas are Distributed Systems and its usage in Workflow Management Systems. I have been studing CORBA and JAVA since 1996 when I implemented a Distributed Domain facility, as an under-graduation project, using OrbixWeb . I am also interested in Object Oriented Programming, CSCW, Groupware and Design Patterns. Now I am implementing a Distributed Architecture for Large-scale Workflow, called WONDER - Workflow on Distributed Environment, as my MS Project.

My main interest areas are: Distributed Systems (WWW, CORBA and Java), Operating Systems (Linux) and Object-oriented programming.
